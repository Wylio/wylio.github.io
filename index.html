Baby Apps

Three simple activities for new humans.

Our first foray into the world of iOS developement.

Peace provides the natural whitenoise of a waterfall to soothe your infant when they're full and ready for sleep but just can't quite find it.

Color lets your slightly older baby use their hands to draw temporary, disappearing, sparkling, lines on top of shifting colors with peaceful string music in the background.

Pop is for children that have learned how to use their hands well enough to appreciate some engaging balloon popping fun with soothing background music that won't annoy parents.

Wylio.com

Creative Commons image search and attribution service.

Wylio.com is a Creative Commons search and attribution service for bloggers. It saves users time by automatically generating platform specific embed code for the millions of free images already available through Flickr.
